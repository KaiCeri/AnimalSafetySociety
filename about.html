About Me

We are Animal Safety Society. We are a UK based company who want to raise awareness, help protect and gather donations to help ensure the safety and welfare of animals around the UK.

We help 1000 animals a month around the UK using our donations, rescuing animals from abusive homes, dangerous climates and rehoming animals.

You can reach out to donate through our contact page. Any money can go a long way.
